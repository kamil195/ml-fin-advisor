How it works
Three-layer intelligence

Each layer is a distinct ML pipeline — classification, forecasting, and recommendation — working in concert to model your unique financial behavior.

01 · CLASSIFY
Transaction Classification
We sort your transactions into clear spending categories using details like store name, amount, and date. You can personalize categories to match how you manage money.
02 · FORECAST
Time-Series Forecasting
We estimate your upcoming spending based on your past habits and monthly patterns, with a 90-day outlook to help you plan ahead.
03 · ADVISE
Interpretable Recommendations
You get practical suggestions that explain what to change and why, including simple "what if" scenarios to see potential savings.
